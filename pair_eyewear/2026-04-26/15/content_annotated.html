Caring for acetate frames

Hand-polished acetate stays looking its best with just a little attention. Use a microfiber cloth to gently remove fingerprints and oils, and rinse the frames occasionally with warm water to keep them fresh. One thing to avoid: harsh chemicals, which can strip the hand-polished finish over time.

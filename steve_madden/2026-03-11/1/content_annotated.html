How to Choose the Right Dressy Sandal for Your Summer Party

Your party, your outfit, your rules. But if you want a little guidance? We've got you.

Match the sandal to the event. The Hadyn's classic white leather suits casual garden parties and daytime celebrations. The Calissi's champagne finish leans dressier - think evening gatherings and polished occasions. Know your vibe before you add to cart.

Think about what you're wearing. White works as a true neutral across bold prints and pastels alike. Champagne adds warmth that pairs beautifully with earth tones, metallics, and warm-toned fabrics. Different lanes - both covered.

Size up for both styles. Order early enough to try them before the event. Wear them around the house once or twice. The Calissi especially benefits from going a half to full size up.

One event or a whole summer? If you attend multiple summer events and want one sandal that covers the widest range of looks, the Hadyn's versatility makes it a strong everyday pick. Want a statement shoe for a special night? The Calissi delivers.

"They are comfortable and perfect to combine with different looks." - Keylin M. (5★)

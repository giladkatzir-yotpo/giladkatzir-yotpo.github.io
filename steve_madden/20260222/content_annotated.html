Comfortable Ankle Boots FAQ

Are these ankle boots really comfortable enough for all-day walking?
They are. Reviewers confirm wearing both the HUTCHH20 and DUSTY for 10+ hour days without discomfort. One DUSTY owner logged 56,000 steps over 2.5 days in New York City - zero foot pain reported.

Should I size up when ordering?
It depends on the style. The HUTCHH20 can run narrow for some - a few reviewers preferred sizing up a half size, while others found their true size worked well. The DUSTY is generally true to size, though those with wider feet may want to size up a half.

Which boot handles wet weather better?
The HUTCHH20 Black, hands down. Its water-resistant construction and lug sole give it a clear advantage on rainy days and wet conditions.

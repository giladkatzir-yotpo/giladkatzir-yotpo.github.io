How to Choose the Right Heels for a Fall Wedding

Two stunning heels. One fall wedding. How do you decide?

The key? Match the shoe to the wedding vibe.

Match the formality:
- For romantic or formal fall weddings - garden estates, candlelit ballrooms, cathedral ceremonies - the Bri's pearl and lace detailing elevates the look with occasion-ready sophistication.
- For versatile modern weddings - rooftop receptions, vineyard celebrations, restaurant venues - the Jypsey's clean strappy lines pair with virtually anything.

Outfit coordination:
Both heels work beautifully with classic fall wedding guest colors: jewel tones, burgundy, emerald, navy. Whether you're wearing a satin midi or a flowing maxi, these silhouettes complement - they never compete.

Comfort planning for the occasion:
Weddings involve sitting, standing, and dancing in bursts. Both heels suit that natural rhythm. The Jypsey has the edge for marathon dancers (see the detailed breakdown above). The Bri is ideal for a gorgeous entrance paired strategically with flats for later.

Sizing - this applies to both styles:
Reviewers consistently recommend sizing up a half to full size. Both tend to run small, especially around the toes. Order early enough to exchange if needed - you don't want to break in new shoes the morning of the wedding.

How to Choose the Right Heel for Your Night

Two incredible heels and one bachelorette party. So how do you decide?

The key? Think about the vibe.

Occasion Type Matters

Whether you're planning a brunch-to-bar crawl with your crew or a downtown dinner that ends at the hottest club - your heel should match the energy.

The BRI WHITE LACE PEARL leans romantic and textured. Think garden-party vibes, rooftop brunches, or any bachelorette with a feminine, whimsical dress code.

The LARINA IVORY SATIN reads sleeker, more modern. Your downtown dinner heel. Your club-night showstopper. A refined silhouette that pairs with clean lines and confident energy.

Outfit Coordination

Lace and pearl pair naturally with white mini dresses and floral prints. The texture creates visual interest - you don't need much else.

Ivory satin? Complements satin slip dresses, tailored jumpsuits, anything with clean lines. Go monochromatic. Let the shoe do the talking.

The Honest Trade-Off

Reviewers consistently praise both heels for impressive comfort at 4 inches. But let's be real - comfort tapers after a couple of hours. These are designed to steal the spotlight for a few fabulous hours, not survive a 12-hour endurance test.

The move? Tuck a cute pair of flats in your bag if your night involves an all-hours marathon. Smart brides plan ahead.

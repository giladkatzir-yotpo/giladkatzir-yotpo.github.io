How to Choose the Right Shoes for a Fall Wedding

Two incredible heels. One wedding invite. Here's how to decide.

Consider the Formality

Whether you're attending an evening cocktail reception with champagne towers or a daytime vineyard ceremony surrounded by rolling hills - formality sets the tone. Gold strappy heels read glamorous and celebratory, ideal for evening events and any reception with a dance floor. Mocha pointed-toe heels work seamlessly from daytime ceremonies to formal ballroom events - that rich, neutral tone adapts to nearly any setting.

Coordinate with Your Outfit

  • Gold pairs with: Jewel tones, navy, black, and deep emerald cocktail dresses - the classic fall wedding guest palette.
  • Mocha complements: Burgundy, forest green, rust, olive, and earth-toned ensembles. Think warm and grounded.

Both colors work with prints, solids, and everything in between. No wrong answers here.

Plan for Comfort

Let's be real. Wedding events involve seated dining, standing for toasts, and bursts of dance-floor energy - sometimes all in one night. Both heels earn strong comfort marks from reviewers during shorter wear periods. But for a full reception that stretches past midnight? Cushioned insoles extend your endurance and your fun.

Think Beyond the Wedding

Pick the shoe you'll reach for again. Both styles have the polished look to transition from wedding guest duty to holiday parties, date nights, and work events. From day to night - without compromising on style.

The key? Pick the one that makes you feel like you. Bold and golden? Warm and grounded? Either way, you're showing up with confidence.

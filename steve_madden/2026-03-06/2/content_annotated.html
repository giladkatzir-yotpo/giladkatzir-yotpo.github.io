Size, Prep, and Style Your Sparkly Heels

You've picked your pair. Now let's make the experience flawless.

Break them in - seriously. Don't skip this.

Some owners report initial tightness or stiffness. Wear your heels around the house for 2–3 short sessions before the wedding. Especially important if you sized up and want to confirm fit with any inserts.

Your shoes, your prep routine. But trust us - a little break-in goes a long way.

A few more practical tips. Adding cushioned ball-of-foot pads makes a real difference for receptions that run long - and stick with a heel height you already know you can handle. A wedding is not the time to test your limits. The heels have a rounded toe and crisscross back design, and that rounded toe gives your toes more room than a pointed style, which helps on longer nights.
